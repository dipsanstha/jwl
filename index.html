About New Madi Jewellers

New Madi Jewellers is a family-owned jewellery store in Bharatpur, Nepal, offering a wide range of gold, silver, and diamond ornaments inspired by Nepali culture and tradition. We are trusted for purity, quality, and fair pricing.

With years of expertise in traditional craftsmanship, we specialize in creating timeless pieces that celebrate Nepal's rich cultural heritage while embracing modern elegance.
